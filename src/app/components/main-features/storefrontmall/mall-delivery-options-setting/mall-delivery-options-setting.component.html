Delivery Options

StorefrontMall offers managed delivery services for any item purchased on your store. The delivery charge is paid by the customer. We handle the pickup from your store and delivery to the customer.

StorefrontMall also allows for you manage your own delivery; in this case when an order is placed; you will be in charge of delivering to the customer. Kindly note its important to update the order status from your sales section; this will allow for the system to confirm delivery to your customer.

Please select from the options below which delivery setup your prefer (only one can be used at a time)

    KudiGo should manage my deliveries for me

  • Merchant Own Managed Delivery
