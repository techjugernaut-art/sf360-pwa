Requirement Information

KudiGo will take a maximum of 1 business day to approve your request to start accepting payments on the Storefront App. This will happen after the KYC documents uploaded are verified to be compliant. However, this process might take longer than the expected approval period should the KYC documents be found to be non-compliant. We advise that care be taken in the provision of all the neccessary documents, required of your business type, to minimize delays.

The Business Type selected when providing your Business Information will determine the KYC documents you will have to upload. Below are the Business Types and its required KYC documents.

KYC For In-Store Card POS Payment

  1. Full Name
  2. Valid Government Issued ID
  3. Phone Number (To be validated via OTP and must bare same name as merchant name on ID)
  4. Business Name
  5. Business Address Location (GPS & Physical Address)
  6. Date Of Birth
  7. Description and nature of business
  8. Business Registration Document
    • Certificate Of Incorporation
    • Certificate Of Commencement
  9. Business TIN (Tax Identification Number)

Settlement (Either bank or mobile Money)

  1. Bank Name
  2. Bank Account Name
  3. Bank Account Number
  4. Mobile Money Wallet Name
  5. Mobile Money Network
  6. Mobile Money Number

KYC For In-Store MobileMoney Payment

  1. Full Name
  2. Valid Government Issued ID
  3. Phone Number
  4. Business Name
  5. Business Address Location (GPS & Physical Address)
  6. Date Of Birth
  7. Description and nature of business

KYC For Online-Store Card & Mobile Money Payment

  1. Full Name
  2. Valid Government Issued ID
  3. Phone Number (To be validated via OTP and must bare same name as merchant name on ID)
  4. Business Name
  5. Business Address Location
  6. Date Of Birth
  7. Description and nature of business
  8. Tax Identification Number (Individual)
  9. Mobile Money Number (Where Applicable)

NB: The primary forms of individual identity verification include:

  • Valid international passport
  • Valid driver’s license
  • National I.D. Card
  • Permanent Voter’s Card

Sole Proprietorship

Registered Business Names; e.g. (Sole Proprietorship, Partnership)

  1. Certificate of Registration of the business
  2. Certified True Copy of CAC/BN/1 (Application for Registration of Business Name)
  3. Certified True Copy of CAC/BN/1 (Application for Registration of Business Name)
  4. Valid Identification document of registered Proprietor(s) or Partnership(s)
  5. Utility Bill;
  6. Special Control Unit against Money Laundering (SCUML) certificate (where applicable)

Non Profit Organization

Charities, Religious Associations, Clubs, Schools, NGOs, Societies & Associations

  1. Certificate of Registration of the Charity/Society/Club/School
  2. Cooperative registration certificate obtained from government registration body (for cooperative societies only)
  3. Certified true copies of Rules/Constitution of the Association or Club or association
  4. Resolution (or minutes of last meeting) authorizing the establishment of business relationship with Interswitch on the letter headed paper, duly signed by at least two principal officers/Trustees and nominating signatories/authorizers
  5. Requisite government approval/gazette in relation to schools
  6. Utility bill
  7. Valid IDs of nominated authorizers; and
  8. SCUML registration certificate with the exception of Religious Organizations

Private Limited Liability

Private Limited Liability Company

  1. Certificate of Incorporation of the company and
  2. Memorandum and Articles of Association [certified as true copy by the Registrar of Companies] and
  3. Form CAC 7 (formerly CO7 - Particulars of Directors) certified as a true copy by the Registrar of Companies and;
  4. Form CAC 2. (formerly CO2-statement of return on allotment of shares) certified as a true copy by the Registrar of Companies and; OR
  5. Certified True Copy of Form CAC 1.1 (Application for Registration of Company) for companies registered in 2017 (Post CAC reform)
  6. Board resolution (signed by at least two Directors or a Director and Company Secretary) authorizing the relationship and introducing representatives/authorizers of future change
  7. Utility Bill;
  8. Valid Identification Documents of all the major shareholder with 5% holding and above;
  9. Operating license (where applicable) for company operating in Nigeria and regulated by the CBN (e.g. Bureau De Change, Payment Solution Service Providers (PSSPs), Mobile Money Operators (MMO), International Money Transfer Operators (IMTOs), Lottery /Gaming etc.)

Public Limited Liability

Public Limited Liability Company

  1. Certificate of Incorporation of the company and;
  2. Memorandum and Articles of Association [certified as true copy by the Registrar of Companies] and;
  3. Certified list of major shareholder(s) with 5% holding and above;
  4. Board resolution (signed by at least two Directors or a Director and Company Secretary) authorizing the relationship and introducing representatives /authorizers of future change
  5. Evidence of listing on the stock exchange (where applicable); and
  6. Operating license (where applicable)
