Product Images Guidelines:

  • Required dimensions 270 x 270 pixels
  • Should be your product image or service included with text information e.g description and product name.

Do's and Don'ts for better Product Images:

DOs

Where possible please follow these DOs to better your Product Images:

  • Do take photos during the daytime
  • Do position the item next to a window with natural light
  • Do keep the product and background clean and simple e.g white background or black background
  • Do use a plain, neutral color surface or table cloth if necessary
  • Do ensure product is sharp and clear
  • Do maintain consistency to give your photos a uniform look
Don'ts

Where possible please follow avoid these Don'ts to better your Product Images:

  • Don't use flash light from your camera
  • Don't take photos at night
  • Don't take pictures under fluorescent lighting
